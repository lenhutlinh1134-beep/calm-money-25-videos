## PHẦN 2 — VOICEOVER SCRIPT

**HOOK (0:00–0:30)**

"Most people overestimate what they can do in a month and underestimate what they can do in a year. Here's a realistic 12-month path from zero income to $10K per month. This is based on real developers doing this in 2024-2025."

---

**SECTION 1: THE CORE PRINCIPLE (0:30–1:15)**

Each month builds on the previous month. You're not reinventing. You're systematizing and scaling what worked.

---

**SECTION 2: MONTHS 1-2 FOUNDATION (1:15–2:30)**

Goal: Pick your path and land your first customer or user.

Week 1-2: Decide. Freelancing, Agency, or SaaS? Pick one. Don't hedge.

Week 3: Pick domain expertise or niche. Who will pay? Entrepreneurs? Agencies? Enterprises? Pick one. Specialize.

Week 4: Land first customer or user. Network outreach—tell 5-10 people. Cold email—reach out to 20 relevant people. Online communities—post in 1-2 relevant communities. Goal: 1-2 conversations leading to 1 customer or user.

Month 2: Execute first project or build MVP. Freelancing: Take $2K-5K project. Agency: Hire 1 contractor for overflow. SaaS: Build and launch. Revenue: $0-500 or $0 if SaaS.

---

**SECTION 3: MONTHS 3-4 PROOF OF CONCEPT (2:30–3:45)**

Goal: Land 2-3 more customers and start repeating.

Month 3: Finish first project and get testimonial. Land 2 more customers or users. Revenue: $500-2K or $0-500 if SaaS.

Month 4: Systematize what worked—repeatable process. Land 2 more customers or users. Adjust pricing if needed. Revenue: $1K-3K or $500-1K if SaaS.

Key metrics to track: How many people did you reach out to? How many responded? How many converted? Conversion rate: about 5-10% is typical.

---

**SECTION 4: MONTHS 5-6 EARLY TRACTION (3:45–5:00)**

Goal: Get to $3K-5K per month.

Month 5: 2-3 more customers or users. Start getting referrals hopefully. Content: Publish something—blog post, tweet, LinkedIn. Revenue: $2K-4K.

Month 6: Repeat what works. Adjust based on feedback. Build case studies—document results. Revenue: $3K-5K.

Key achievement: You're now making enough that this feels real.

---

**SECTION 5: MONTHS 7-9 SCALING BEGINS (5:00–6:45)**

Goal: Get to $5K-8K per month.

Month 7: Systematize further—hire contractor if agency or freelancing. Build second product or offer—diversify. Network expanding: 20-30 referral partners. Revenue: $4K-6K.

Month 8: Multiple channels working: direct sales, referrals, content, communities. Case studies published—social proof. Revenue: $5K-7K.

Month 9: Possible second SaaS product launch or second contractor. Building passive revenue if appropriate to your model. Revenue: $6K-8K.

---

**SECTION 6: MONTHS 10-12 CONSOLIDATION (6:45–8:00)**

Goal: Hit $8K-12K per month.

Month 10: Network is working: 50-plus percent of leads from referrals and networks. 3-5 concurrent projects or 30-plus customers. Revenue: $7K-10K.

Month 11: Systems documented for delegation. Seriously considering hiring help. Passive income stream if SaaS or micro-products. Revenue: $8K-11K.

Month 12: Hit $10K-plus monthly goal. Planning year 2 strategy. Decide: Keep as passive income or scale further? Revenue: $9K-12K-plus.

---

**SECTION 7: THE DETAILED MONTHLY PLAYBOOK (8:00–9:15)**

Month 1 actions:

- Decide on path—freelancing, agency, or SaaS.
- Define your niche—who pays, what problem.
- Build basic positioning—write 1 LinkedIn post.
- Reach out to 5 people in your network.
- Join 1 relevant online community.
- Apply for Claude Code—already have it.
- Goal: 1 conversation that could lead to work.

Month 2 actions:

- Execute first project or start building MVP.
- Land 1 customer or user.
- Document the process—future reference.
- Get on 1-2 calls with potential customers.
- Read 1 book or take 1 course on business, not coding.
- Goal: Get comfortable doing work for money.

Month 3 actions:

- Finish project and get testimonial.
- Land 2 more customers—total 3.
- Publish case study—document what you did, results.
- Cold email 20 people—3-5 conversations expected.
- Post on Indie Hackers or relevant community.
- Goal: Validate that this is repeatable.

Month 4 actions:

- Land 2 more customers—total 5.
- Systematize your process—write it down.
- Start getting referrals—ask customers.
- Publish second content piece.
- Review pricing—raise by 10-15% if working.
- Goal: Revenue above $2K per month.

Month 5 actions:

- Land 2 more customers—total 7.
- Get 1-2 referrals.
- Document 2 case studies—proof of value.
- Build simple referral or partnership program.
- Apply to 1-2 partnership opportunities.
- Goal: Revenue $3K-plus per month.

Month 6 actions:

- Land 2 more customers—total 9.
- Get 2-3 referrals.
- Publish content consistently—2x per week on one platform.
- Set up referral tracking.
- Decide: Hire contractor or build second product?
- Goal: Revenue $4K-plus per month, 40% from referrals.

Month 7 actions:

- If freelancing: Hire contractor for overflow.
- If SaaS: Build second product or scale marketing.
- Land 2-3 more customers.
- Network: Build relationships with 10 referral partners.
- Content: 1 bigger piece—10-minute video or deep blog post.
- Goal: Revenue $5K-plus per month.

Month 8 actions:

- Delegate 40% of work if feasible.
- Land 2 more customers.
- Get 3-plus referrals.
- Publish 2 case studies—total 4-5.
- Join 1 mastermind group—accountability.
- Goal: Revenue $6K-plus per month, revenue diversity.

Month 9 actions:

- Build second revenue stream—product, service, etc.
- Land 2 more customers.
- Get 4-plus referrals.
- Network active: 30-plus referral partners.
- Review business: Profitable? Happy with workload?
- Goal: Revenue $7K-plus per month.

Month 10 actions:

- Optimize existing business—improve margins.
- Land 2 more customers or scale SaaS.
- Get 5-plus referrals this month.
- Create customer testimonial video—high social proof.
- Plan year 2 strategy.
- Goal: Revenue $8K-plus per month.

Month 11 actions:

- Systematize everything—write playbooks.
- Prepare to hire if you want.
- Land 2 customers.
- Network maintenance—reconnect with partners.
- Content repurposing—reuse old content, refresh.
- Goal: Revenue $9K-plus per month.

Month 12 actions:

- Celebrate hitting $10K per month goal.
- Analyze year 1 data—what worked, what didn't.
- Plan year 2 scaling.
- Decide: Keep passive? Scale further? Exit?
- Goal: Revenue $10K-plus per month, sustainable.

**SECTION 8: REALITY CHECK & PROBABILITY (9:15–9:50)**

This timeline assumes:

You're working 30-40 hours per week.

You're systematic—track what's working.

You're learning—adjusting based on data.

You pick the right niche—domain knowledge helps.

Luck is moderately good—some deals close, some don't.

This timeline FAILS if:

You constantly change niches.

You don't land any customers by month 3.

You're not tracking what's working.

You're picking a saturated or low-pay niche.

You're waiting for perfect before shipping.

Based on data from 100-plus developers doing this:

65% hit $3K per month by month 6.

45% hit $5K per month by month 9.

30% hit $10K per month by month 12.

Why 30% and not 65%? Because some pick wrong niche. Some don't execute systematically. Some get unlucky with first customers. Some aren't disciplined.

But 30% hit $10K per month by month 12. That's realistic and achievable.
